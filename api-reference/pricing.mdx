EigenExplorer is currently free to use and open to the public. All users start with our `Hobby` tier plan, which includes:

<Note>The Hobby tier will remain free forever with these limitations.</Note>

-   1,000 free requests per month
-   Maximum rate limit of 100 requests per minute
-   No credit card required

### Coming in Q1 2025: Paid Plans

We will be introducing additional pricing tiers in Q1 2025 that will include:

-   Increased usage limits
-   Additional API routes
-   Enhanced features

Detailed pricing information and tier comparisons will be available closer to launch.

### Need More Requests?

If you require a higher request allowance before the introduction of paid tiers, please contact our [support team](https://t.me/+siHEtj56TNdmZmU1) to discuss your needs.
